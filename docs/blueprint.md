# **App Name**: Adat & Cinta

## Core Features:

- Elegant Event Details: Clearly display all pertinent wedding information including date, time, specific location with a map link, and important notices.
- Couple's Photo Gallery: Showcase a high-quality, beautifully presented photo gallery of the couple, allowing guests to view their journey.
- Interactive Guestbook: Allow guests to leave personalized messages, well wishes, and greetings for the couple.
- AI Blessing Tool: An AI-powered tool to help guests compose thoughtful and culturally-inspired Batak blessings or messages for the guestbook.
- RSVP Management: Provide a simple and elegant way for guests to confirm their attendance for various wedding events.
- Add to Calendar: Enable guests to easily add the main wedding event details directly to their digital calendars.

## Style Guidelines:

- A deep, rich maroon primary color (#661929) to convey elegance and tradition.
- A very dark, subtle background (#1F191D) to provide depth and make the primary and accent colors pop, contributing to a luxurious feel.
- A radiant gold accent color (#E1B231) used for highlights, calls-to-action, and key ornamental elements to enhance luxury.
- Headlines and prominent text use 'Playfair' (serif) for an elegant, high-end, and artistic aesthetic, drawing inspiration from classical fashion typography.
- Body text and supporting information use 'PT Sans' (humanist sans-serif) to ensure readability and a modern complement to the headline font.
- Utilize minimalist, sophisticated icons, potentially integrating subtle Batak traditional motifs or patterns for an authentic touch without being overly ornate.
- Clean, spacious layouts with strong visual hierarchy, incorporating ample whitespace to emphasize content and photography. The overall design should feel symmetrical and balanced.
- Subtle, smooth animations for transitions between sections or when photos load, enhancing the app's elegance without distracting from the content.
